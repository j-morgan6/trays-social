# Frequently Asked Questions

Short, plain-language answers to the things people ask most. For the formal
details, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy), [Terms of Service](/terms), and
[Community Guidelines](/community-guidelines).

## What is Trays?

Trays is a recipe-sharing community for home cooks. You post recipes — a photo,
the ingredients, the steps, and the tools you used — and they appear in the
feeds of the cooks who follow you, newest first. You can like, comment, follow
other cooks, and save recipes to your tray to cook later. There is no algorithm
deciding what you see; you see the cooks you chose to follow.

## Who is it for?

Home cooks — anyone who likes making food and wants to share what they cook or
discover what other people are actually making. You do not need to be a
professional or a food blogger. Trays is rated 13+.

## Is it free?

Yes. Trays is free to use. There are no payments, no subscriptions, and no
in-app purchases.

## Do you have ads?

No. Trays has no advertising. Your feed is the cooks you follow, in order — not
sponsored posts or promoted content.

## Do you track me?

No. We do not run third-party analytics, we do not collect advertising
identifiers, and we do not track you across other apps or websites — so there
is no App Tracking Transparency prompt. We collect only what is needed to run
the service (your account info, the content you post, and basic server logs).
The full breakdown is in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

## How do I report a problem?

If a post or a cook breaks our [Community Guidelines](/community-guidelines),
use the report option on the post, or block the user to stop seeing their
content and hide yours from them. For anything else — a bug, a safety concern,
or a question — email us at [support@trays.app](mailto:support@trays.app) and we
will look into it.

## How do I delete my account?

Open **Settings** and choose **Delete account**. This permanently removes your
account and your content. Deletion is honored within 30 days, and backups are
purged within 90 days, as described in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy). If you
have trouble, email [support@trays.app](mailto:support@trays.app).

## Where does my data live?

Your account data, posts, and comments are stored in a Postgres database hosted
by Fly.io in Toronto, Canada. Recipe photos are stored with Tigris Data. We do
not sell your personal information. Note that posts, recipes, comments, and
profiles are public by default and can be viewed on the web — see
[Privacy Policy](/privacy) §7 for exactly what is public versus private.

## How do I contact support?

Email us at [support@trays.app](mailto:support@trays.app). We read every
message.
